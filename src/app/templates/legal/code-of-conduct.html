AI/ML Professional Community Bangladesh

Code of Conduct

AI/ML Professional Community Bangladesh is a volunteer-led, non-profit community. Our focus is on strengthening the local AI/ML community and ensuring a free, open-source, and collaborative space for professional growth. We do not charge learners or pay mentors.

Community Goal

  • Knowledge Sharing: Encouraging open-source contributions, sharing research, projects, and tutorials.
  • Mentorship & Support: Organizing Q&A sessions, workshops, and mock interviews to help early-career AI/ML practitioners.
  • Community Engagement: Facilitating discussions and arranging meetups.
  • Research & Development: Promoting study groups and research validation.

Code of Conduct

  1. Be Respectful & Inclusive
    • Treat all members with respect regardless of background or opinion.
    • No discrimination, harassment, or hate speech.
  2. Collaborate & Share Responsibly
    • Share accurate, open-source content with proper credit.
    • Avoid plagiarism.
  3. Support & Encourage
    • Give constructive feedback.
    • Be patient with beginners.
  4. No Spam, Promotions, or Self-Promotion
    • Avoid excessive self-promotion.
    • Projects are welcome if they provide community value.
  5. Keep Discussions Professional
    • Stay on-topic in discussions.
    • Resolve disagreements respectfully.
  6. Respect Privacy & Rules
    • Do not share personal info without consent.
    • Follow moderator instructions.
  7. Conflict of Interest
    • Do not violate NDAs or confidentiality agreements.
  8. Avoid Political Discussions
    • Focus only on AI/ML and professional topics.

Applies to: All contributors, members, admins, guests, speakers, organizers, and event attendees.

Terms & Conditions

By joining and participating in AIML Professional Community Bangladesh, you agree to the following terms:

1. Consent for Recording & Publishing

  • You consent to being recorded during events.
  • Recordings may be shared on community platforms.
  • Turn off your camera or mic if you prefer not to be recorded.

2. Content Sharing & Intellectual Property

  • Only share content you have rights to.
  • Do not share confidential information.
  • Give proper credit in all open-source contributions.

3. Privacy & Data Protection

  • Do not share others' private info without consent.
  • We may collect minimal info (email/LinkedIn) for organizing events only.

4. Limitation of Liability

  • We do not offer job placement, legal, or financial services.
  • Advice given is based on personal experience, not professional liability.

5. Amendments & Updates

  • Terms may be updated as needed.
  • Members will be notified of major changes.
